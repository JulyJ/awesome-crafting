### Weeding Tips for Intricate MyLaserTools Designs

Connected text and detailed designs require careful weeding:

| Challenge | Solution | Tool | Time Saving |
|-----------|----------|------|-------------|
| **Tiny letters** | Use bright light, magnification | Weeding tool, lamp | Better precision |

## Troubleshooting Cricut-Specific Issues

| Issue | Symptom | Cause | Fix |
|-------|---------|-------|-----|
| **Tearing during cut** | Cardstock rips | Dull blade or wrong setting | Replace blade, adjust pressure |
| **Not cutting through** | Incomplete cuts | Low pressure or thick material | Increase pressure, double cut |
| **Mat won't stick** | Material shifts | Worn mat | Re-sticky mat or replace |
| **Vinyl won't weed** | Tiny pieces stick | Too much pressure | Reduce pressure slightly |
| **Design too small** | Can't weed details | SVG imported too small | Resize in Design Space before cutting |
