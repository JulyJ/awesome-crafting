## Common Challenges & Solutions

### Beginner Troubleshooting

| Problem | Why It Happens | Quick Fix | Prevention |
|---------|---------------|-----------|------------|
| **Yarn Won't Feed** | Tension issues, thread path | Check threading, adjust tension | Follow manual threading exactly |
| **Uneven Pile Height** | Inconsistent gun angle | Keep gun perpendicular | Practice consistent positioning |
| **Backing Rips** | Fabric too loose or tight | Adjust frame tension | Taut but not stretched |
| **Yarn Pulls Out** | Insufficient backing | Double-check backing method | Use quality latex/glue |
| **Gun Jams** | Yarn knots, wrong thickness | Clear pathway, check yarn | Pre-wind yarn smoothly |
| **Patchy Coverage** | Rows too far apart | Tuft closer together | Follow 1-2mm spacing rule |
