## The Rise of Tech-Enhanced Crafting

The boundary between traditional crafts and technology has dissolved, creating a vibrant new category: smart crafts. This emerging trend combines the warmth of handmade items with the innovation of electronics, appealing to both tech enthusiasts and traditional crafters seeking to elevate their creations.

## Safety & Best Practices

### Electrical Safety for Crafters

| Safety Concern | Risk Level | Prevention | What to Know |
|----------------|------------|------------|--------------|
| **Low Voltage (5V-12V)** | Very Low | Use proper power supplies | Safe to handle, unlikely to shock |
| **Soldering Burns** | Medium | Soldering stand, ventilation | Hot iron can burn, use fume extractor |
| **Battery Safety** | Medium | Don't short circuit, proper storage | LiPo batteries can catch fire if damaged |
| **Heat from LEDs** | Low-Medium | Heat sinks, spacing | High-power LEDs get hot over time |
| **Power Supply Mismatch** | Medium | Check voltage/current ratings | Wrong voltage damages components |
| **Fire Hazard** | Low | Don't leave powered on unattended | Rare but possible with failures |

### Quality & Reliability

| Best Practice | Why It Matters | Implementation | Professional Result |
|---------------|----------------|----------------|---------------------|
| **Solder Connections** | Prevents failures | Use flux, heat properly | Reliable, long-lasting |
| **Strain Relief** | Protects wires | Secure cables, avoid tension | Durable construction |
| **Waterproofing** | Outdoor/humid use | Conformal coating, sealed enclosures | Weather-resistant |
| **Code Comments** | Future troubleshooting | Document what code does | Maintainable projects |
| **Power Calculation** | Prevents overload | Calculate total LED draw | Safe, stable operation |
| **Testing** | Catch issues early | Test before final assembly | Functional guarantee |
