## Troubleshooting for Young Crafters

### Common Issues & Kid-Friendly Fixes

| Problem | Why It Happens | Easy Fix | Prevention Tip |
|---------|---------------|----------|----------------|
| **Clay Cracking** | Drying too fast or unevenly | Smooth with water, slow dry | Cover with plastic while working |
| **Paint Won't Stick** | Clay not fully dry or dusty | Wait 48 hours, wipe dust | Test dryness before painting |
| **Too Soft/Sticky** | Fresh clay, warm hands | Refrigerate 10 minutes | Work in cooler environment |
| **Too Hard/Crumbly** | Clay drying out | Add water, knead thoroughly | Store in airtight container |
| **Uneven Thickness** | Inconsistent rolling | Use guides (popsicle sticks) | Roll from center outward |
| **Pieces Won't Stick Together** | Dry clay surfaces | Roughen edges, add water | Score and slip technique |
| **Bubbles After Painting** | Air trapped in paint | Thin paint layers, let dry between | Apply thin, multiple coats |
